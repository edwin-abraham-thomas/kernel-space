## Editing Tips

- Anchor cuts to voiceover meaning, not just beats
- Hold on real system footage long enough to feel credible, not long enough to bore
- Use Remotion compositions as connective tissue between screen footage
- Avoid heavy transitions; prefer hard cuts and text overlays
- If a clip is weak, replace it with a cleaner alternative rather than stretching the weak one
- Let the trailer energy come from contrast, timing, and real footage, not overdesigned motion
